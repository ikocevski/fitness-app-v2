CubeFit Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 9, 2026

CubeFit ("we", "our", "us") provides fitness coaching features including workout plans, nutrition guidance, and progress tracking. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Account information, such as name and email address.
  • Fitness data you provide, such as weight logs, workout progress, and diet-related entries.
  • Basic technical data needed for app performance and security.

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To provide workout and nutrition features.
  • To support coaching workflows and progress tracking.
  • To maintain app security, reliability, and performance.

Third-Party Services

CubeFit uses third-party infrastructure providers, including Supabase (data/authentication) and Bunny.net (media delivery). These providers process data on our behalf to operate the app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with service providers required to run the app and where required by law.

Data Retention

We retain personal data while your account is active and for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

Your Choices

  • You can delete your account directly inside the CubeFit app.
  • You can request account deletion and data removal by contacting us.
  • You can update certain profile information within the app.

Children's Privacy

CubeFit is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

Contact Us

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact us at: thecube123@icloud.com

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
